The Problem

Is your website holding your business back?

Most business websites were built to exist — not to perform. If any of these hit close to home, you're exactly who we built Mimic Studio for.

01
"My site looks outdated."

First impressions are formed in 50 milliseconds. An outdated site signals to customers that you don't care about detail — even if you do.

02
"Traffic comes in. Nobody converts."

Getting visitors means nothing if they don't become leads. Weak CTAs, confusing layouts, and no trust signals are conversion killers.

03
"My competitor looks more legit."

Perception is everything. If their site looks sharper than yours, customers assume they're better — regardless of the truth.

04
"I built it myself and it shows."

DIY websites get you started, not ahead. They're a ceiling, not a floor — and they hold your business back faster than you realise.

05
"It doesn't work on mobile."

60%+ of your visitors are on a phone. A site that breaks on mobile isn't just embarrassing — it's actively losing you business daily.

06
"I have no idea if it's even working."

No analytics, no clarity, no visibility. If you can't measure it, you can't improve it — and nothing changes.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do you work with brand new businesses?
Absolutely. We work with businesses at every stage — from pre-launch startups to established companies looking to fix an underperforming site. If you're just starting out, we'll help you build a strong foundation from day one.
How long does a website project take?
The Starter package typically delivers in 7–10 business days. The Growth package takes 14–18 business days. Custom projects are scoped individually. Timelines are kept tight because we work on one project at a time.
Do I need to provide the content and copy?
You can if you'd like to — but it's not required. We'll guide you through a simple brief and help structure your messaging. For Growth and Custom projects, we offer light copy guidance to make sure your words actually convert.
Will my website work on mobile and tablets?
Yes, without exception. Every website we build is designed mobile-first. We test across multiple devices and screen sizes before anything goes live. Over 60% of web traffic is mobile — we treat it as the primary experience, not an afterthought.
What do you need from me to get started?
Just fill in the contact form below. We'll schedule a free 30-minute audit call to understand your business, your goals, and what's not working with your current site. From there, we'll recommend the right package and send a clear proposal.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes. The Growth package includes 30 days of post-launch support as standard. Beyond that, we offer monthly maintenance retainers covering updates, performance monitoring, and ongoing improvements. Ask us about retainer pricing during your audit call.
