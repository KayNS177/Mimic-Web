Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

This policy explains what personal information Mimic.Studio ("we", "us") collects through mimicstudio.co, how we use it, and the choices you have.

When you submit our quote form, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, company name (optional), and the details of your project. If you contact us via WhatsApp or email, we receive the contact details and message content you send.

We also use Vercel Analytics and Vercel Speed Insights to understand site traffic and performance. These tools collect aggregated, anonymized usage data (such as page views, country-level location, device type, and performance metrics) and do not use cookies to track you across sites.

How we use your information

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your enquiry, prepare a quote, communicate about your project, and deliver our services. We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

How your data is processed

Quote form submissions are delivered to us via EmailJS, an email delivery service. Our website is hosted on Vercel. These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy and security commitments.

Data retention

We keep enquiry and project correspondence for as long as needed to serve you and meet legal or accounting obligations, after which it is deleted.
